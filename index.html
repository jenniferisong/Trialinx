The Problem

Budget negotiations are broken across the industry

Every clinical trial site reviews protocols and negotiates budgets differently. Without a shared benchmark, sites leave money on the table, sponsors overpay in some places and underpay in others, and the whole process takes months it shouldn't.



  • 📋
    No consistency across sites Each coordinator has their own spreadsheet, their own FMV guesses, and their own negotiation style. The result is wide variance in outcomes for identical work.
  • 🕐
    Months of back-and-forth Budget negotiation is a manual, iterative process with no shared reference point. Reasonable discussions turn into extended email chains.
  • 📊
    No real market data Sites rely on memory or legacy benchmarks. There's no live dataset of what similar procedures actually cost across comparable trials.

How It Works

From protocol to negotiation-ready in minutes

Trialinx reads your documents, structures the data, and surfaces the insights that matter — so your team can focus on the conversation, not the preparation.

01

Upload Your Documents

Drop in a protocol, budget grid, or sponsor proposal. Trialinx parses the visit schedule, procedure lists, and cost line items automatically — no manual data entry.

The Platform

Everything your team needs to negotiate confidently

🔍 Protocol Intelligence

AI reads dense protocol documents and extracts the visit schedule, procedures per visit, and any requirements that affect site workload and cost — in seconds, not hours.

📊 Fair Market Value Benchmarking

Every line item is compared against aggregated data from comparable trials. You see the actual market range, not a guess — with source context for each estimate.

🚩 Automatic Flagging

The system highlights procedures that are under-priced, missing from the budget, or inconsistent with protocol requirements — so nothing slips through review.

✉️ Draft Sponsor Responses

Ask the AI assistant to generate justification text for any budget change request. Get professional, data-backed language that strengthens your negotiating position.

📁 Version Tracking

Keep a clean record of every budget iteration, what changed, and why. Build institutional knowledge that makes every future negotiation faster.
